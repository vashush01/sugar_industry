Who are we

Established in 1932, Rai Bahadur Narain Singh (RBNS) Sugar Mill, Laksar is one of Uttarakhand’s leading sugar manufacturers, with a crushing capacity of 12,000 TCD. With a rich legacy spanning over nine decades, we have been at the forefront of the sugar industry, consistently delivering excellence in production, sustainability, and innovation. At RBNS Sugar Mill, we are committed to enhancing the lives of farmers, empowering our workforce, and contributing to the growth of India’s sugar sector. Our state-of-the-art infrastructure and adherence to best industry practices ensure high-quality sugar production while maintaining environmental responsibility. Driven by our core values of integrity, efficiency, and sustainability, we focus on optimizing production processes, reducing waste, and leveraging modern technology to maximize output. We work closely with sugarcane farmers, providing them with advanced agricultural support, fair pricing, and timely payments, fostering long-term partnerships that drive mutual growth. Beyond sugar production, RBNS Sugar Mill is actively exploring opportunities in ethanol production, cogeneration, and sustainable energy solutions, aligning with the government's vision for a self-reliant and eco-friendly sugar industry. As a trusted name in the Indian sugar sector, we continue to uphold our legacy while embracing innovation to shape a prosperous future. RBNS Sugar Mill, Laksar - Sweetness With Trust ♥

Our Products

White Sugar

Refined and pure white sugar for your daily needs. BCML is among the leading sugar manufacturers in India with a crushing capacity of 80,000 tonnes of cane per day across 10 plants. All our 10 sugar plants are in Eastern and Central part of Uttar Pradesh.

Power

The company produces green power as it uses its waste product (bagasse) as raw material instead of using fossil fuels which is harmful for the environment. The Company has a cogeneration capacity of 278.47 MW.

Ethanol

Unrefined and full of nutrients. Ethanol is a green fuel produced by the Company which is blended with petrol for use in vehicles. All its Ethanol producing units are running on Zero Liquid Discharge technology. Molasses which is a by-product is used for producing Ethanol & Alcohol.
